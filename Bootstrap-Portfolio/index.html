About

T. A. M, commonly pronouced as TAM is an acronym which was coined by this portfolio's author to depict the one-word-phrase TheAlternateMethod, whose abbreviation and clause is a personal freelancer trade mark and software development slogan respectively.

As a one-man Mobile & Web Application Developer, the author can blend graphics and graphic design software to business logic in order to create a look for a system's interface. It will then be followed by the building of the backbone, typically from the ground up. This explains why the author can develop a responsive mobile & web application and facilitate the creation of an exceptional functional design to effectively bridge the communication gap between interested clients.
